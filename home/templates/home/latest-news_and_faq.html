1 - How long does shipping to Lagos, Nigeria, take?

.Transit times vary based on the shipping method, but air freight typically takes 5-7 days, while sea freight can take 4-6 weeks. We provide estimated delivery dates upon booking.

2 - What items can I ship to Lagos??

You can ship most personal and commercial goods, including household items, electronics, and business supplies. However, restricted or prohibited items like hazardous materials, perishable goods, and certain chemicals require special approval.
